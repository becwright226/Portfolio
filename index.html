About Me

From Back of House to Back-end . . .

My journey to Tech began unexpectedly, and like many great realizations in my life, it came over dinner. Growing up, I always considered myself a creative person, the 'artsy' type. Because of this 'assumption' I tended to steer clear of more 'logic' based studies and focused on what seemed to come most naturally, the Arts.

I took the Arts to a professional level in my adulthood. I explored possibilities in Anthropology, Museum Studies, and Pastry Arts before settling into a career as a Pastry Chef. From 2019-2021, I worked in Pastry and began to realize what I loved most about my job was: the ability to create, find practical solutions to problems, working in a fast-paced/ constantly changing enviornment, the ability to learn everyday, and to work with a diverse group of professionals that push you both professionally and personally.
